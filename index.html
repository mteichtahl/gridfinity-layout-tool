Gridfinity Planner & Layout Tool

A free Gridfinity planner for making bins and laying out drawer organizers. Runs in your browser — nothing to install.

Plan a Gridfinity Drawer Organizer

Built for makers organizing tool drawers, workshop benches, kitchen drawers, parts bins, and craft storage. Measure the drawer, drag to place bins, generate custom STL files, and export a print list. The drawer planner handles tool-drawer organization, drawer dividers, deep drawers with stacked layers, and any drawer shape that fits a 42mm grid.

Bin Generator

The built-in bin generator lets you set dimensions, pick a base style, add compartments, and download the file as STL, STEP, or 3MF.

Other features: wall cutouts, scoop ramps, label tabs, honeycomb wall patterns, and a pen tool for drawing freeform cutouts.

Baseplate Generator

Generate custom baseplates sized to your drawer. Configure magnet holes, connector nubs, and per-side edge padding. Large baseplates split automatically to fit your print bed. Export as STL, STEP, or 3MF.

Layout Planner

Measure your drawer, set the grid size, and drag to place bins. The tool prevents overlaps and shows how everything fits. Layers handle deep drawers, categories add color coding, and the 3D preview shows your layout from every angle.

When you're done, export a print list with every bin size and estimated filament usage.

Who Uses This

  • Makers organizing tool drawers, workshops, and benches
  • 3D printing enthusiasts storing nozzles, filament samples, and parts
  • Anyone with a messy drawer and a 3D printer

How It Works

  1. Measure your drawer in millimeters and divide by 42 to get grid units
  2. Set the drawer size and drag to create bins
  3. Generate custom bins or find STLs on Printables, Thangs, or MakerWorld
  4. Export your print list and start printing

Common Questions

Is it free? Yes. It's free and doesn't require an account.

Do I need to install anything? No. It runs in your browser. You can also install it as an offline app.

What file formats does the generator support? STL for most slicers, STEP for CAD editing, and 3MF for color and material support.

Where do I find STL files for specific bins? The bin generator creates standard bins. For specialized holders, search Printables, Thangs, or MakerWorld for "Gridfinity" plus the size you need.
